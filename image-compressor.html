Image Compressor

Upload an image, adjust quality/resize, preview and download the compressed version.

Supports: JPG, PNG, WebP, AVIF
Tip: Lower quality + resizing give the best file reduction. Use WebP/AVIF for modern browsers.

How to compress images?

1. Lossy compression (JPEG/WebP/AVIF): reduces quality to shrink size.
2. Resize dimensions: reduce width/height to lower pixel count.
3. Reduce color depth or remove metadata (EXIF).
4. Use modern formats (WebP/AVIF) for better quality/size tradeoff.
5. Server-side tools: sharp, imagemagick, or imagemin for batch processing.
